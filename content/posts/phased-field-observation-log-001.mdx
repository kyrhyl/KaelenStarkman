Observation began at 03:42 near the Arzad stargate approach vector.

Local was quiet. D-scan showed industrial traffic, two combat hulls, and a corpse can someone had named *SAINTS STILL BURN*. I was preparing to move when the field bloomed.

Orange lattice. Six visible anchor points. A seventh probable point obscured behind gate wash. Duration: nine seconds. The shape reminded me of a net being pulled through water by a hand too large to see.

The sensors disagreed with each other.

Gravimetric suite called it nothing. Magnetometric suite recorded a spike sharp enough to leave a diagnostic scar. The visual buffer caught the bloom but dropped frames four through six. Audio translated the interference into a low tone that made my teeth ache.

I have seen Amarrian theater. I have seen Republic countermeasure ghosts. This did not feel like either.

The archive cares about facts, so here are facts:

| Field | Reading |
| --- | --- |
| Duration | 9.2 seconds |
| Local count | 7 capsuleers |
| Gate status | Stable |
| Signal color | Orange / amber |
| Repeat event | Not yet observed |

I dropped a marker and left before curiosity became stupidity.

If the field returns, I will be there with better instruments and a worse sense of self-preservation.
